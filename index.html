Pioneered Google Ventures 5 Day Sprints within the OPS

Along with Damian Sura, Sr. Manager, LTC Digital Garage, pioneered the first use of Google Ventures 5 Day Sprints with the Ministry of Labour for the Self-Audit initiative.

The sprint gives teams a shortcut to learning without building and launching products. The sprint is a 5-day process for answering critical business questions through design, prototyping, and testing ideas with customers. Working together in a sprint, you can compress months of time into a single week. The sprint gives you a superpower: fast-forward into the future to see a finished product and customer reactions, before making any expensive commitments.

Recent Code Monkey Work: Keeprite Energy Calculator
